Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CartShare ("we", "us") collects and uses information through the CartShare browser extension and website.

What We Collect

We collect the following information when you use CartShare:

  • Account information: username and email address provided at registration.
  • Purchase data: product name, URL, price, image, and platform (Amazon or AliExpress) of items you save to your list.
  • Authentication token: stored locally in your browser to keep you logged in.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide the CartShare service — saving your purchases and sharing them with friends you choose.

We may in the future sell or share aggregated or individual purchase data with third parties for commercial purposes. This includes product names, prices, and shopping platform information saved through the extension. We will not sell your email address or password.

Data Sharing

We do not currently sell your data to third parties. In the future, we may share purchase data with partners or buyers. If we do, we will update this policy.

Your public purchases are visible to your friends on the platform by your choice.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers. We do not guarantee indefinite retention and may delete inactive accounts.

Your Rights

You may delete your account and purchases at any time through the dashboard. To request complete data deletion, email us at ofergold16@gmail.com.
